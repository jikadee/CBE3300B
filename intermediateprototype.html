We designed and 3D-printed a custom housing to encase the prototype board, TDS sensor circuit components, battery, and pump. This enclosure improves portability and protects components during operation.

3. Filtration Unit Design

We have started designing a new 3D-printed filtration unit. While the general structure remains consistent with our earlier models made from plastic cups, the new design features separable top, bottom, and filtration chambers for improved modularity and ease of maintenance.
