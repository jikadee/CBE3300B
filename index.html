Water Purifier Project | CBE 3300B

Project Overview

This project focuses on designing an efficient water purification system to provide safe drinking water in areas lacking access to clean water sources.

Preliminary Design Report

Objectives

  • Design a portable water purification unit.
  • Ensure removal of common contaminants and pathogens.
  • Maintain affordability and ease of use.
